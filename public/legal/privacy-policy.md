## 2. Data Roles: Controller vs. Processor

Understanding who is responsible for personal data is critical to this Policy.

### 2.1 When We Act as Data Controller

Adam Shriki LTD is the **Data Controller** for:

- **Staff Account Data:** Information provided by tournament staff (Floor Managers, Tournament Directors, Dealers) when creating and managing their LynxPoker accounts.
- **Technical Data:** Data automatically collected from all users for security, performance, and product improvement purposes.

### 2.2 When We Act as Data Processor

Adam Shriki LTD is the **Data Processor** for:

- **Player Data:** Personal information about poker tournament players that is entered into the Service by authorized tournament staff on behalf of the tournament organizer (the "Customer").

The **Customer (Tournament Organizer) is the Data Controller** for all Player Data. The Company processes Player Data solely on the Customer's behalf, in accordance with the Customer's instructions and applicable law.
